Contract Vehicles

Main Sail holds multiple contract vehicles that streamline procurement and accelerate delivery for federal and state government clients.

Main Sail's established contract vehicles reduce acquisition timelines and simplify procurement for government agencies at every level. By leveraging these pre-competed, competitively awarded contracts, our clients gain rapid access to Main Sail's full spectrum of IT consulting, engineering, and program support services -- without the overhead of a lengthy open-market solicitation.

Navy Contract Vehicle

SeaPort-NxG

Main Sail is a prime contractor on the U.S. Navy's SeaPort Next Generation (SeaPort-NxG) multiple-award contract vehicle. SeaPort-NxG is the Navy's primary vehicle for acquiring support services across 23 functional areas in engineering, technical, and programmatic disciplines.

Contract Details

Contract Number N00178-19-D-8061
Ordering Agency Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA)
Period of Performance January 2019 – January 2029

Task Order Areas

Functional Area Description
Engineering & Technical Support Systems engineering, design, and technical analysis
Program Management Schedule, cost, and risk management services
Research & Development Applied research and technology development
Logistics Support Integrated logistics and sustainment engineering
Information Technology Network, cybersecurity, and software engineering
